About Emma's Eats

Emma's eats is inspired by picky kids everywhere. This menu is exclusively for kids and toddlers who only eat the same 6 meals day and night. Here is where you can relax while your kids eat a meal you could easily make at home for a lot cheaper. You can bask in the natural light and relaxed ambiance while screaming children throw french fries at your face. We offer complimentary peanut butter facials by the resident toddlers. Enjoy a hot cup of coffee and scroll through social media while your kids scream your name and throw their food on the floor. We hope you truely enjoy the experience here because while you can't leave your children unattended, we take care of the mess and the dishes! It's a small vacation for moms everywhere!
